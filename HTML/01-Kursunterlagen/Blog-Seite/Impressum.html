Hello! My name is Elif Berra, and I’m both a student and an apprentice. In my free time, I love reading.
Some of my favorite authors include George Orwell, Franz Kafka, Stefan Zweig, and Friedrich Dürrenmatt. On the other hand, I’m not a fan of Goethe and Schiller—I find them way too overrated.
Aside from reading, I enjoy watching anime.
So far, I’ve watched Demon Slayer (just the first season, since Netflix only has that one), Tsubasa, The Promised Neverland, Haikyuu, and a few others that I can’t quite recall at the moment.
And now, for my favorite part—my cats!
I have two incredibly smart, cute, and well-behaved cats. The first is named Alpha, and the second is Aykiz. We named the first one Alpha because, after my family adopted her, I noticed that
she’s unusually social for a cat. In psychology, the term "Alpha" describes a leader, someone who is confident and good at speaking, which perfectly fits her personality.As for the second one,
her name is Aykiz, which is Turkish. Translated literally, it means "moon girl." My mother chose the name, though to be honest, I’m not really sure why she picked it! I wanted to give her the name Beta actually.
